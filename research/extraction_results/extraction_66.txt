# Summary of Nollywood-Related Information

This is an interview with actor **Yemi Solade** from PM News, **July 2, 2004**, discussing various aspects of the Nigerian movie industry (Nollywood).

## Key Topics Discussed:

**Use of English Actors in Yoruba Movies:**
- Solade opposes casting non-Yoruba speakers in Yoruba films due to language bastardization
- He criticizes producers who prioritize commercial profit over language preservation
- Specific actors mentioned:
  - **Zack Orji** - criticized for not speaking Yoruba at all
  - **RMD** - featured in "Ayomida"
  - **Emeka** - noted as speaking Yoruba
  - **Rachael Oniga** and **Ngozi Nwosu** - praised for speaking flawless Yoruba

**Industry Issues:**
- Poor movie sales attributed to market proliferation
- Too many producers entering the industry without proper standards
- The industry described as still in "teething problems" phase, not yet fully developed

**Financial Disparities:**
- English movie producers pay significantly higher fees than Yoruba producers
- English producers attach greater importance to an actor's celebrity status and business value

**Caucus System:**
- Exists in the Yoruba movie industry as a cultural practice of mutual assistance among practitioners
- Described as beneficial to the industry's growth
