 \n\n**The Ban and Affected Parties:**\nThe Association

 cited a high level of indiscipline in the movie industry as justification.\n\n**Industry Response:**\n-
  Movie marketers and producers have begun scouting for raw talent on university campuses to replace the
   banned stars with actors willing to abide by their rules and accept lower fees.\n- Key industry players 
   generally support the ban, viewing the affected artistes as having become \"too powerful and swollen-headed.\"\n- 
   An anonymous actor stated: \"It serves them right,\" questioning the meaning of the G8 designation.\n\n**
